# 13. Out of Scope and Future Considerations

## Purpose
This document defines explicitly excluded functionality and potential
future enhancements to avoid scope creep.

## Out of Scope (Current Phase)
- Online booking by clients
- Online payments and payment gateways
- Inventory management
- Marketing and CRM automation
- Multi-branch support

## Future Considerations
- Client self-service booking
- Integration with payment providers
- Advanced financial analytics
- Loyalty and discount programs

## Notes
Out-of-scope items may be revisited in future roadmap phases.
